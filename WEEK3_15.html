What Is Creative Design?

Creative design

It is a process that uses digital and physical image-making tools to create renders and representations used for marketing purposes.

Creative design as a field relies heavily on the work of visual designers but is not entirely limited to it.A creative designer produces artwork and design concepts that can take on many different forms. For example, you might create visuals, brainstorm concepts, work with team members to develop concepts, and ensure that the final product is aligned with the company or client standards.
